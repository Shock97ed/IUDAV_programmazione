About Us

Powerlust is known for its unique way of handling game development,as the studio doesn't have a producer in either of their teams.

The work culture at Powerlust is very different to many other studios;there's a lot less middle-management; the studio's lead effects artist, Luigi Good, said: "It comes with a lot of pros and cons but I think it definitely is one of our biggest strengths. Looking around at the rest of the industry, and this is something that we do talk about quite a bit, the companies that are doing really innovative, cool things are all the ones that don't have the management, like the business side, directly injected into the company. We don't have any interactions with other software house at all on the development."Powerlust has also complete freedom in basically every aspect of game design.

The standard game design model of Powerlust is to establish the plot, characters and art of the game first. Battle systems, field maps and cutscenes are created next. A typical game of the company involves a team of 4 people. Powerlust doesn't usually use other companies' engines, preferring to code from scratch. According to Romeo Lifely, Powerlust has settled into this game making model since Limbo in 2010.
