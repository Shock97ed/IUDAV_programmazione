OUR STUDIOS

Powerlust Studios is a software house located in Italy, was founded in 2014, from an idea of 4 young boys: Luigi Buono, Dario Casolaro, Romeo Vitale and Roberto Cuozzo. Our studios are known for its latest works on Firewatch, Transistor and Limbo, three multi-awarded games, widely acclaimed around the world.

HOW WE WORK

During these early years, our studios have gained a rich co-operation with the most influencing figures of the modern gaming, like Camposanto, Supergiant Games or Microsoft Game Studios.Our workstation is divided in four section, each assigned to one of the 4 founders, in which we work on the different phases of the game developement.
